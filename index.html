A Little About Me

I'm a recent graduate who fell for the web the way some people fall for a good café — slowly, then all at once. What began as curiosity about how things work grew into a love for writing clean code and crafting interfaces that feel warm, considered, and easy to settle into.

I care about the small details: the unhurried animation, the comfortable spacing, the moment of discovery when everything just feels right.
